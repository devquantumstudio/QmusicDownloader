About This Project

Last updated:

Qmusic Downloader is a free Application that helps you fetch downloadable audio links from supported music platforms. It’s built to be fast, simple, and clean—no accounts, no ads, and no cookies.

What We Believe

  • Free & non-monetized: You can use the app without paying. Donations are optional and never required.
  • Privacy first: We don’t use cookies (including third-party cookies), trackers, analytics beacons, or push notifications.
  • Minimal footprint: Lightweight UI, no pop-ups, no clutter.

How It Works

  1. Paste a music track URL in the input box.
  2. Select the audio format.
  3. Click Download. The download link will appear below once it's ready.

Tip: Some links may take a few seconds to process, especially from larger platforms. Please keep the tab open while it works.

Supported Platforms

  • Amazon Music
  • Amazon Store
  • Apple Music & iTunes
  • Deezer
  • SoundCloud
  • YouTube & YouTube Music
  • Spotify
  • Audiomack
  • Pandora
  • Tidal

What We Don’t Do

  • No ads, no third-party cookies, no tracking.
  • No user accounts and no personal data collection.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of user data—ever.

Respectful & Lawful Use

Use this tool only for music and audio content that you own or are legally allowed to download. You are solely responsible for how you use the app and for complying with the laws and platform terms that apply to you.

Prohibited: illegal downloads, copyright infringement, distribution of unauthorized content, or any use that violates applicable laws or terms. We do not support or condone misuse.

Donations

This is a community-driven project provided free of charge. If you find it helpful, feel free to support us with a voluntary donation via our donation page. Donations are optional and not payments for services.

App & Tech

  • Runs as a standalone application—no browser required.
  • Fully self-contained: no plugins, extensions, or add-ons needed.
  • Cross-platform: works on Windows and Android.
  • Privacy-respecting: no accounts, analytics, or third-party ads.
