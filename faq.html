Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last updated:

1. How do I download music?

  1. Copy the URL of the music track from a supported platform.
  2. Paste it into the input box.
  3. Select your preferred audio format.
  4. Click Download and wait for the link to appear.

2. What audio formats are available?

We support common formats like MP3, M4A, WEBM, AAC, FLAC, OPUS, OGG and WAV.

3. Is this app free to use?

Yes! Qmusic Downloader is completely free to use. No registration, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees. Donations are optional and never required.

4. Are there any download limits?

No. You can download as many tracks as you'd like. However, processing large or long tracks may take a few extra seconds.

5. Do I need an account to use this?

No account is needed. We don’t require any login or personal information.

6. Is it private and safe?

Yes. We don’t use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools. No data is stored or shared. Downloads happen directly, and your privacy is fully respected.

7. Which music platforms are supported?

We currently support the following platforms:

  • Amazon Music & Amazon Store
  • Apple Music & iTunes
  • Deezer
  • SoundCloud
  • YouTube & YouTube Music
  • Spotify
  • Audiomack
  • Pandora
  • Tidal

8. Why do I get an error when trying to download?

Sometimes the download process may fail on the first attempt due to temporary processing issues. If you encounter an error, simply try again — in most cases, it works perfectly on the second attempt.

9. Is it legal to download music?

You should only download music you own or have permission to download. Downloading copyrighted content without authorization may violate laws or platform terms. You're responsible for ensuring lawful use.

10. Can I download full albums or playlists?

Currently, the tool is optimized for single-track URLs. Support for albums or playlists may be added in the future.

11. How can I support the project?

You can make a voluntary donation via our donation page. Donations help keep the project free and community-driven.

12. Who can I contact for support or questions?

For feedback, questions, or support, visit our Contact page. We’ll try to respond as soon as possible.
